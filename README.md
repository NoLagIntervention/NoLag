# NoLag+
NoLag+ is the solution to all your lag problems in your Private SMP and more!

We are a group of coders who have dedicated our time to provide a better gaming experience for you. This used to be a private plug-in/mod but we decided to make the plug-in version public as we are confident that it runs smoothly. 
We are currently running on NoLag+ v4.1.1 if you are interested in using the plug-in for your server, dm me on discord @ RaiTheShroom#0129. I will personally come and visit your SMP and if we like it, we might even play on it!
